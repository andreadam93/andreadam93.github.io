# AI for Aerospace Systems

I apply and extend **Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs)** and **Theory of Functional Connections (TFC)** to aerospace domains, emphasizing real-time control and on-board learning.

Projects include:
- Neural network-based control synthesis verified via Lyapunov stability.
- Data-driven estimation of regions of attraction using Zubov equations.
- Hardware-in-the-loop testing for embedded real-time control with NVIDIA Jetson and Raspberry Pi.
