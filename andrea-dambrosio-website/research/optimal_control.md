# Optimal Guidance and Control

I investigate the integration of **machine learning frameworks** with **functional interpolation techniques** to address open- and closed-loop optimal control problems.

Key projects include:
- Development of **Pontryagin Neural Networks (PoNNs)** and **Bellman Neural Networks (BeNNs)** for open- and closed-loop optimal control.
- Stability and convergence analyses of neural network-based controllers.
- Application to missile interception, orbital transfers, landing, and rendezvous.
