# Research Overview

My research explores the intersection between **machine learning** and **astrodynamics**, focusing on two main areas: **Space Situational Awareness (SSA)** and **Optimal Guidance and Control**.

Below are some of my main research directions. Click each to learn more.

## Research Areas

### [Space Situational Awareness](./ssa)
Monitoring and characterizing artificial space objects using **Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs)** and **Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs)** to infer trajectories, attitude states, and maneuvers from telescope data.

### [Optimal Guidance and Control](./optimal_control)
Integrating functional interpolation techniques and neural networks (such as **PINNs** and **Extreme Learning Machines**) for efficient and accurate solutions to optimal control problems.

### [AI for Aerospace Systems](./ai_aerospace)
Developing data-driven frameworks for **autonomous spacecraft**, **lunar landing**, and **real-time trajectory optimization**.
