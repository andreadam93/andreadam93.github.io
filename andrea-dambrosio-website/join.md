# Join the Lab

I welcome motivated undergraduate, master's, and Ph.D. students interested in topics such as **astrodynamics**, **optimal control**, and **AI for aerospace systems**.

If you are interested in joining, please send me an email with:
- A short statement of your research interests.
- Your CV.
- Any relevant coursework or experience.

Opportunities will be posted here as they become available.
