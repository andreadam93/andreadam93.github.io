Lab director

Andrea D’Ambrosio, Ph.D.

Assistant Professor of Aerospace Engineering · Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering · University of South Florida

Director since 2026

Astrodynamics, autonomy, and intelligent space systems.

Dr. D’Ambrosio’s research focuses on astrodynamics, space situational awareness, optimal guidance and control, orbit determination, space-object behavioral analysis, space-environment modeling and evolution, certification of machine-learning-based controllers, and machine learning and optimization for aerospace.

Previously, he held postdoctoral research positions in the Space Systems Engineering Laboratory at the University of Arizona and the Astrodynamics, Robotics, and Controls Laboratory at MIT.

He earned his bachelor’s degree in aerospace engineering, master’s degree in space and astronautical engineering, and Ph.D. in Astronomy, Astrophysics, and Space Science from Sapienza University of Rome.
