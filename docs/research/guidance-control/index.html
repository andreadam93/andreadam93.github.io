Research area

Optimal guidance & control.

Fast, physically consistent methods for constrained trajectory optimization, inspection, landing, and small-body proximity operations.

Astrodynamics & space situational awareness →Optimal guidance & control →Identification of nonlinear dynamics →

Pontryagin and Bellman Neural Networks

Pontryagin Neural Networks solve the boundary-value problems arising from indirect optimal-control methods and the Pontryagin minimum principle, including path constraints. Lagrange multipliers and Fischer–Burmeister complementary-slackness conditions are embedded in physics-informed loss functions.

The Extreme Theory of Functional Connections approximates latent solutions while analytically satisfying boundary constraints. Bellman Neural Networks provide a complementary physics-informed framework for optimal-control problems with integral quadratic cost.

Periodic inspection missions

Periodic close-encounter orbits repeatedly survey a target spacecraft for civil and defense applications. CIRO designs these orbits using constrained n-impulse maneuvers, genetic algorithms, and particle-swarm optimization while accounting for encounter distance, maneuver cost, illumination, daily observation time, perigee, eccentricity, and repetition-time requirements.

Soft lunar landing with hazard avoidance

Real-time trajectory replanning combines particle-swarm optimization with differential flatness to produce analytical control policies whose boundary and dynamic constraints are satisfied by construction.

Hazard detection and safe recovery maneuvers address cratered terrain and optimization failures. The complete approach has been evaluated through Monte Carlo simulation and a robotic hardware-in-the-loop facility with a camera and simulated lunar surface.

Proximity operations around single and binary asteroids

Research spans reference-orbit tracking, adaptive control, PSO-based optimal control, binary-asteroid transfers, solar-sail hovering, surface hopping, and safe circumnavigation. Particle-swarm optimization, RRT* path planning, and neural models trained through Extreme Learning Machine support operations in weak and irregular gravity.
