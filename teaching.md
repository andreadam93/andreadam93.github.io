# Teaching Philosophy

I aim to cultivate curiosity and enthusiasm in students while bridging the gap between theory and real-world aerospace problems.

My approach combines **peer instruction**, **retrieval practice**, and **hands-on projects**, encouraging students to think critically and apply knowledge independently.

### Courses of Interest
- Astrodynamics
- Orbital Mechanics and Attitude Control
- Optimal Guidance and Control
- Space Robotics
- AI for Aerospace Systems

### Teaching Methods
- Interactive lectures using Jupyter Notebooks and MATLAB Live Editor.
- Group projects with peer review and presentations.
- Focus on inclusivity, accessibility, and adaptive teaching.
